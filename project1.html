Description

AuVault is an Android app that a team and I developed using Thunkable. The app is tailored to users who are in the jewelry industry and provides a scrap gold calculator and inventory management system to track their assets.

The scrap gold calculator features the current gold value in grams and ounces. Additionally, it allows users to change the units from grams to ounces with the press of a button.

The inventory system allows users to take a picture of their asset, as well as the set an id number, enter the weight, carat weight, the total cost of the good, retail value, gold purity, and bin number.

Skills Learned

The skills that I learned during this project, were app development and design, team coordination, and project management.
